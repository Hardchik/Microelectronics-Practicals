Steps to see the simulations
1) You can download the Simulations.zip file and extract it.
 
2) Download LTspice in your computer and make sure you open the 
applications with .asc extension with LTspice to view the simulations.

3) When LTspice is open, then click on the Simulations button in toolbar and 
then click on 'Run' to start the simulations.


Open the Simulations (LT spice) folder to see the simualtion files with (.asc) extension.
Open the Images folder to view images regarding simulations
